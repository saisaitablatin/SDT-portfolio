About Me

I'm a UI/UX Designer with a passion for creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences. Fueled by my love for K-Pop, particularly EXO, their music inspires my creativity and design approach. With a dream to travel the world, I seek to infuse global perspectives into my work, making each design unique and user-friendly.

Portfolio

Cultura: A Global Events Discovery App

This project involves designing 'Cultura', a mobile app that connects users with cultural events worldwide. The challenge is to create an intuitive and engaging user interface that allows users to easily discover, save, and share events like music festivals, art exhibitions, and traditional ceremonies. The UX aspect will focus on a seamless user journey from discovering events to attending them, while the UI will emphasize an aesthetic that captures the global diversity of cultures. This project will demonstrate skills in user research, responsive design, and creating a cohesive visual language that resonates with a diverse user base.

HealthMate: Personal Wellness Tracker

Design 'HealthMate', a comprehensive health and wellness app tailored for individuals seeking a balanced lifestyle. The project involves creating an engaging and accessible interface that allows users to track various aspects of their health, including diet, exercise, sleep, and mental well-being. The UX challenge is to ensure a personalized and motivational journey for each user, while the UI focus will be on a clean, calming, and easy-to-navigate design. This project will showcase skills in user-centric design, data visualization, and crafting a digital experience that encourages positive health habits.
